Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts and has since evolved into one of the premier technology conferences worldwide. Over the years, TechCon has hosted thousands of attendees, featuring groundbreaking talks and innovative workshops.

Our Mission

TechCon's mission is to inspire, educate, and connect the global tech community. We strive to foster innovation, promote diversity, and empower the next generation of technology leaders.

Notable Past Speakers

Ada Lovelace

Renowned as the first computer programmer, Ada Lovelace delivered an inspiring keynote on the future of computational thinking at TechCon 2018.

Grace Hopper

A pioneer in computer science, Grace Hopper shared her insights on programming languages and innovation at TechCon 2019.

Tim Berners-Lee

Inventor of the World Wide Web, Tim Berners-Lee discussed the evolution of the internet and its impact on society at TechCon 2022.
